Driver Controller
-------------------
Left Joystick - Forward/Backward/Left/Right
Right Joystick - Stationary Rotation
A - Nudge Down
B - Nudge Right
X - Nudge Left
Y - Nudge Up
Left Button - Nudge Counter-clockwise
Right Button - Nudge Clockwise
Right Trigger - Boost

Gunner Controller
-------------------
A - Tote Bottom Preset
B - Tote Middle Preset
Y - Tote Top  Preset
Left Button - Tote Arm Up
Right Button - Tote Arm Down
Left Trigger - Can Arm Up
Right Trigger - Can Arm Down
