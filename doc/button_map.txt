Driver Controller
-------------------
Left Joystick - Forward/Backward/Left/Right
Right Joystick - Stationary Rotation
A - Nudge Back
B - Nudge Right
X - Nudge Left
Y - Nudge Forward
Start - Left Backwards Turn
Back - Right Backwards Turn
Left Button - Nudge Counter-clockwise
Right Button - Nudge Clockwise
Left Trigger - Boost
Right Trigger - Slow


Gunner Controller
-------------------
Left Joystick(Tote):
   Press - Tote Bottom Preset
   Down - Tote Level 1
   Left - Tote Level 2
   Right - Tote Level 3
   Up - Tote Level 4
Right Joystick(Can):
   Press - Can Bottom Preset
   Down - Can Level 1
   Left - Can Level 2
   Right - Can Level 3
   Up - Can Level 4
B - Stop Lifter
Y - Toggle Kicker
Start - Tote Level 5
Back - Can Level 5
Right Button - Go To Engage Kicker Height For A Tote On The Ground
Left Trigger - Tote Top Preset
Right Trigger - Can  Top Preset
